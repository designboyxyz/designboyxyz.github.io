aware

aware is an android-based application to help u.s. air force commanders keep track of soldier health metrics in real-time. the app is compatible with any bluetooth heart-rate monitor. sessions can be logged for offline analysis.

i designed, programmed, and tested the app.

anfacio

anfacio is the best way to make new friends (on android). i used google firebase to set up an app that let people find others with similar interests as them, near their location. users search through a list of profiles, and can narrow their results using a filter. results are anonymous, users can share moods, colors, and interests publically, but can only share pictures in the chat.

i designed, programmed, and tested the app. it was launched as a beta.
